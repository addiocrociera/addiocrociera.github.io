ABOUT

Tim Iseler is an audio engineer currently based in Durham, NC. A former employee of Soma Electronic Music Studios in Chicago, IL, Tim has been recording, mixing and producing records for over seventeen years. Additionally, he has traveled the world as a live sound engineer, tour manager and production manager since 2005.


Please contact him with any questions or for scheduling and rate information.
